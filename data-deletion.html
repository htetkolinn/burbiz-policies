Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: April 7, 2025

How to Request Data Deletion

At BurBiz, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You have the right to request the deletion of personal data that we have collected about you.

This page explains how you can exercise your right to request deletion of your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws and our Privacy Policy.

How to Submit a Deletion Request

You can request deletion of your personal data in any of the following ways:

Email Request

Send an email to chipmunk.ster.romen@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"

Contact Form

Use our contact form and select "Data Deletion Request" from the dropdown menu

Written Request

Send a written request to:
BurBiz Data Protection
No550, Zaya Street, South Okkalapa
Yangon, Myanmar

Information to Include

To help us process your request efficiently, please include:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your BurBiz account
  • Business name (if applicable)
  • A clear statement that you are requesting deletion of your personal data
  • Any additional information that might help us identify your data

Our Response Process

  1. We will acknowledge receipt of your request within 3 business days
  2. We will verify your identity to ensure we are responding to the right person
  3. We will process your request and delete applicable personal data
  4. We will send you confirmation once the deletion is complete

We aim to complete all deletion requests within 30 days. If we need more time, we will notify you.

Data We May Retain

In some cases, we may need to retain certain information for:

  • Legal compliance and regulatory requirements
  • Fraud prevention and security purposes
  • Completing transactions you requested before deletion
  • Exercising or defending legal claims

Any data we retain will be handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
